YOKAI RACING

Yokai Racing was founded by me, Walter Jones, who lives for the art of speed and the love of motorsport. What began in the streets soon moved onto the tracks, where a handful of drivers obsessed with speed and the perfect lap helped turn Yokai into a successful racing organization.

Here at Yokai Racing, we believe every car has a soul and every driver is the heartbeat. Our mission is and always will be to make them beat in unison. We are the ghosts in the machine, the "Yokai" that haunt the apex of every turn. We bring a level of professional artistry to the track that transforms every race into a singular manifestation of speed. Through clean execution, the team works tirelessly to stay in front of the pack.
